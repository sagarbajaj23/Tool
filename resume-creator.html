Resume Writing Tips

1

Tailor Your Resume for Each Job

Customize your resume for each position you apply for. Highlight the skills and experiences most relevant to the job description.

2

Use Action Verbs

Start bullet points with strong action verbs like "developed," "managed," "increased," or "implemented" to demonstrate your impact.

3

Quantify Achievements

Where possible, include numbers to quantify your accomplishments (e.g., "Increased sales by 20%" or "Managed a team of 5 developers").

4

Keep It Concise

Aim for 1-2 pages maximum. Be selective about what you include and focus on recent, relevant experiences.

5

Proofread Carefully

Typos and grammatical errors can get your resume rejected immediately. Proofread multiple times and consider having someone else review it.
